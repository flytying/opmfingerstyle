# OPM Fingerstyle — Product Definition

## Product type
Niche content platform + curated guitarist directory + gear discovery website.

## Core concept
OPM Fingerstyle is a website focused on **Pinoy fingerstyle guitarists performing OPM songs**.

The platform allows guitarists to have a public profile containing:
- photo
- bio
- YouTube channel link
- selected video links
- tablature links
- social links
- contact info

The platform also includes:
- gear/product pages
- editorial content pages
- browse/listing pages

## Primary user value
Users can:
- discover Pinoy fingerstyle guitarists
- watch performances
- find tabs
- explore gear related to fingerstyle playing

## Business model
Revenue comes from:
1. product promotion placements
2. affiliate links
3. AdSense

## Product positioning
This is:
- a curated niche media/content platform
- a guitarist discovery platform
- a gear discovery platform

This is not:
- a social network
- a marketplace for promoted profiles
- a tab hosting platform
- a messaging platform

## Core product insight
Traffic pages:
- guitarist pages
- song/tab pages
- blog/guide pages

Money pages:
- gear/product pages
- buying guide pages
