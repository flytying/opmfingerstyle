# OPM Fingerstyle — SEO and Content Strategy

## SEO model
The site grows through three content pillars:

### 1. Directory SEO
Pages:
- guitarist profiles
- tab listings
- optional song pages

### 2. Editorial SEO
Pages:
- guides
- best-of articles
- beginner content
- gear recommendations
- comparisons

### 3. Internal linking
Rules:
- profiles link to tabs and videos
- articles link to profiles and gear
- gear pages link to related artists or playing styles

## Example content topics
- best OPM fingerstyle guitarists
- beginner OPM fingerstyle songs
- best guitars for fingerstyle in the Philippines
- best strings for warm acoustic fingerstyle tone
- fingerstyle guitar accessories for beginners
- featured Pinoy fingerstyle arrangements

## SEO publishing rules
- do not publish thin pages
- do not publish empty profiles
- require minimum useful content
- prioritize quality over page count

## Content strategy rule
Profile pages bring trust and discovery.
Guide and gear pages bring stronger monetization.
