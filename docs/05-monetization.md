# OPM Fingerstyle — Monetization Specification

## Monetization priority
1. product promotion placements
2. affiliate links
3. AdSense

## Monetization philosophy
Monetization must feel native to the niche and must not reduce trust.

## Page-type monetization rules

### Guitarist profile pages
Purpose:
- discovery
- artist credibility
- long-tail SEO

Allowed monetization:
- one light contextual product module
- one small affiliate block
- light AdSense only if it does not clutter

Disallowed:
- aggressive sponsor wall
- multiple heavy product sections above the artist content

### Gear/product pages
Purpose:
- highest commercial intent
- strongest affiliate opportunity

Allowed monetization:
- product placements
- affiliate links
- sponsor modules
- product comparisons
- light AdSense

These pages are the main money pages.

### Song/tab pages
Purpose:
- search traffic
- contextual monetization

Allowed monetization:
- relevant accessory suggestions
- affiliate links related to style or setup
- light AdSense

### Blog/guide pages
Purpose:
- SEO acquisition
- convert informational intent into commercial clicks

Allowed monetization:
- affiliate links
- sponsored modules
- AdSense
- internal links to gear pages

## Product categories
Initial monetization categories:
- acoustic guitars
- classical guitars
- strings
- capos
- tuners
- pickups
- microphones
- audio interfaces
- amps
- stands
- cables
- accessories

## Sponsored product rules
Sponsored placements must:
- be clearly labeled
- be relevant to the page
- not overpower editorial content
- appear in limited quantity

## Affiliate rules
Affiliate blocks must:
- use disclosure where required
- support fallback to normal external URL
- be manageable in admin
- remain contextually relevant

## AdSense rules
AdSense is supporting revenue only.

Avoid:
- too many ads above the fold
- cluttered profile pages
- poor mobile readability
